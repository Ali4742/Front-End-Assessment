Xbox

Overview

Xbox Series X is the latest console released. At £479, it is around the same price as the PS5. It offers better hardware than the Xbox One and faster loading time. With Xbox Game Pass, you get access to a large collection of exclusive games.

It supports graphics up to 4K and 120 FPS and backwards compatible Xbox 360 games

Specs

  • Processor: AMP Zen 2
  • Memory: 16GB RAM
  • Storage: 1TB SSD
  • Resolution: 4K 120 FPS
  • Cost: £479

Pros

  • 120 Hz refresh rate
  • 2TB storage is plenty for a few games
  • Performance mode means faster frame rate

Cons

  • Disc drive is not included with console
  • Higher price than the original consoles
  • Need a good TV for full advantage
