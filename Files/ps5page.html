Playstation

Overview

Playstation 5 (PS5) is the next generation console. It offers more powerful hardware than the Playstation 4 and faster loading times. However it costs more than the Xbox at £499

The new DuelSense controller included vibration and adaptive triggers to adapts to the state of the state of the game you are playing

Specs

  • Processor: AMD Rizen 3
  • Memory: 20GB RAM
  • Storage: 2TB SSD
  • Resolution: 4K 120 FPS
  • Cost: £499

Pros

  • 120 Hz refresh rate
  • 2TB storage is plenty for a few games
  • Performance mode means faster frame rate

Cons

  • Disc drive is not included with console
  • Higher price than the original consoles
  • Need a good TV for full advantage
