Nintendo Switch

Overview

The Nintendo Switch is a hybrid console that can be used as a handheld or docked to a TV. It is cheaper than the Xbox and Playstation costing £279. Great for gamers that want a modern console at a bargain price

However, it has weaker hardware compared to the PS5 and Xbox.

Nintendo offers exclusive game such as Mario Kart and The Legend Of Zelda.

Specs

  • Processor: AMP Zen 2
  • Memory: 16GB RAM
  • Storage: 1TB SSD
  • Resolution: 4K 120 FPS
  • Cost: £279

Pros

  • 120 Hz refresh rate
  • 2TB storage is plenty for a few games
  • Performance mode means faster frame rate

Cons

  • Disc drive is not included with console
  • Higher price than the original consoles
  • Need a good TV for full advantage
